Discography

Szysztof has no discography in the old sense of the word. Well, he didn't release anything on vynil, CDs or other disc-shaped media (hard drives of online platform servers and the single, most likely lost CD of "Осколки" album don't count) and is unlikely to do so in the foreseeable future.

But in the new sense of the word, the discography not only exists, but is also divided into official (released through the distributor on a large number of platforms) and unofficial (released manually on one or two platforms).
