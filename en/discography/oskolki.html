Information

Detailed history of the album "Осколки" ("Shards") is presented in these two videos in Ukrainian with grimaces and stupid pictures, but otherwise quite informative.

Recording started on November 21, 2009, prelast day of quarantine due to swine flu pandemic. Three songs ("О том, что", "Закат" and "Лекции-инфекции") were recorded that day, music and lyrics being created on the fly without pre-takes and cuts. Nylon guitar became the main accompanying instrument, because it doesn't need no cable, its sound doesn't need no setup - just grab and play!

Despite of the low quality of the recordings, spontaneous method of songwriting seemed much more appealing to Szysztof* than the swamp of perfectionism he was drowning in before**. Thus it was decided to record a whole album using the same method. Moreover, all the tracks were deleted immediately after mixing them to song, both due to the small capacity of the hard drive to which they were recorded and to suppress the desire of enhancement and cleaning.

Since studies at the university have resumed, and since the inspiration does not choose when to come: at home, on the road or in class, "Оковы твои" and "Глупая песня" became the last songs to be composed right at the time of recording (although the recording of the former was considered unsuccessful and was not included in the album). The subsequent ones were composed in a conventional way - on pieces of paper, which only improved their quality. Songs were still recorded from the first take though.

Exact dates of creation of most of the songs have been lost because the computer to which they were recorded had dead real-time clock battery. Only their order is known: "Ты ушла" (first loud, then quiet version), "Аполитичный хмырь", "Осколки" (first lively, then sad version), "...и не спрашивайте...". Only after the listed songs "Оковы твои" was finally recorded.

The song "Нелепая история" was recorded on January 16, 2010 and is the most detailed description of the relationship between Szysztof* and the girl to whom most of the album’s songs are dedicated and who became the author of the drawing for its cover.

The recording of the album ended on January 21, 2010 with the song "Что ещё светит?".
