Information

The song "Lies Against Lies" was originally intended for the album "НуЩоЦеЗа". Written under the impression of the second round of the 2019 presidential elections in Ukraine. Published the day after the vote, when votes were still being counted, but the winner was already obvious. In an attempt to hype (not very successful), Szysztof called the video "Вибори 2019 навіяли пісню" ("Elections 2019 in Ukraine reflected in the song").

The text of the chorus was written much earlier, in 2016 or in the first half of 2017, as an imitation of an Ukrainian folk song. It is no longer possible to remember what the melody was back then. Most likely, more monotonous, slow-paced and creepy.
